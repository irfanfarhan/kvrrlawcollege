Management Details

Sri.K.Pratap Reddy

Biography

  • "Senior Advocate” in the High Court of A.P.
  • Member of Bar Council of A.P. for two terms./li>
  • President of the A.P. High Court Bar Association
  • Chairman of the Commission to enquire into the alleged atrocities of Maoists and the Police excesses – 1997 – Report accepted by Government and ordered by the High Court to be implemented.
  • Trustee of several organizations running educational institutions in twin cities and the State.
  • On the Trust Board of Mahila Abhyudaya Trust.
  • Associated with many minority educational institutions like Deccan Medical College and Deccan Engineering College, Anwar-ul-Uloom College, Muffakam Jah Engineering College and Madina Educational Society as a the lawyer and legal adviser.
  • A Human Rights Activist, Socialist and associated with ‘Citizens for Democracy’ (CFD) Founder-member of Janata Party
