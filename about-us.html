About Us

K.V. Ranga Reddy Law College, Hyderabad established in the year 1991 is one of the leading premier law colleges in Hyderabad. K.V.R.R. Law College is named after a legendary personality Late Sri Konda Venkata Ranga Reddy, Former Dy. Chief Minister, State of A.P., and he was popular as a Statesman, Educationist, Politician with high rectitude and values, Social Worker and Reformer and in whose name one District is carved out in the State of Telangana i.e., Ranga Reddy District. The College is situate in the heart of the city in a memorable academic ambience with a large campus and good infrastructure, and is run by A.V. Education Society, Hyderabad, running both 3 years and 5 years law course. Well, talking about the campus, it’s pretty huge for a college in the city; with about 10-12 acres of land the campus is pretty cool and breezy most of the times. The ground is huge and maintained well. There’s cricket ground, volleyball court, and indoor games too. All this is common and shared by the entire campus. The library is very resourceful; all the books needed are available. The library also has subscriptions to National & International law generals, newspapers, and legal magazines so that you can keep updated with the latest happenings.Internet access is also available at the library. The college also has a nice auditorium where events like debates or moot courts can be conducted. Apart from that, students say that every minute spend in the campus is lively and enjoyable.

  • An institution striving for excellence in legal education with values.
  • Centrally located in an ideal academic environment.
  • Provided with good infrastructure, i.e. spacious classrooms, good furniture and an attractive Moot-Court Hall.
  • Well equipped with good library, books, journals & Magazines, Computer lab.
  • Efficient and Senior teachers committed & devoted to the cause of standard legal education.
  • Special efforts to train Telugu medium students in learning English and law.

Our Vision

KV Ranga Reddy Law College envisions promoting quality education and research through interdisciplinary understanding, state-of-the-art learning, and the use of emerging knowledge for developing world-class human resources capable of mastering the global challenges of future technology and management. KV Ranga Reddy Law College seeks to create, preserve, and disseminate knowledge to build competitive capability for holistic development of man and society.

Our Mission

To prepare a class of proficient scholars and professionals with ingrained human values and commitment to expand the frontiers of knowledge for the advancement of society.
