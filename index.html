Welcome K V RANGA REDDY LAW COLLEGE

K.V. Ranga Reddy Law College , Hyderabad established in the year 1991 is one of the leading premier law colleges in Hyderabad. K.V.R.R. Law College is named after a legendary personality Late Sri Konda Venkata Ranga Reddy, Former Dy. Chief Minister, State of A.P., and he was popular as a Statesman, Educationist, Politician with high rectitude and values, Social Worker and Reformer and in whose name one District is carved out in the State of Telangana i.e., Ranga Reddy District.
